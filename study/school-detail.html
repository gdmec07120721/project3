    • History

  • Insworld has been offering a British-style education in Singapore since its establishment in 2001.
  • Insworld was officially declared open by the former British High Commissioner, His Excellency, Sir Alan Collins, CMG, on April 23rd, 2004.
  • It has been offering Pearson Edexcel GCE Examinations since its establishment. Throughout that time it has achieved consistently high results.
  • Awarded with Edutrust Provisional Certificate.
  • International acclaim : '“Comparable to the top 25% of schools in the UK” (Mr Lawrence Denholm, Senior Consultant, Airton Bridge Consultant, UK).
  • 'One of the best International Schools in 2005, 2006, 2007, 2008' (excerpt from Newsweek).

    • Courses

  • English for Academic Study (EAS)
  • English for Academic Study (EAS) programme is an intensive integrated programme that allows you to rapidly improve your English in the shortest possible space of time. Equal emphasis is placed on the four key skills of: Listening, Speaking, Reading and Writing. The EAS course lasts for a maximum of nine months, divided into three terms.
  • Junior Secondary (EITP)
  • The Edexcel International Tuition Programme (EITP) is an intensive modular Junior Secondary School Programme. The EITP curriculum incorporates the bestelements of the UK National Curriculum with the real life experience of studying in an international environment.
  • High School (International GCSE Level)
  • The Pearson Edexcel ‘Advanced’ Level Programme at Insworld is an intensive programme that is usually taught over 18 months and divided into six terms. However, the duration can vary depending on the student's entry qualifications.
  • Junior College (Edexcel Pearson GCE Advanced Level)
  • The Pearson Edexcel ‘Advanced’ Level Programme at Insworld is an intensive programme that is usually taught over 18 months and divided into six terms. However, the duration can vary depending on the student's entry qualifications.
  • Pearson BTEC Diploma in Business Level 3
  • This diploma is equivalent to A levels and can be completed in 18 months. This course is for students who are interested in a business degree or a career in business and would prefer a more criteria based qualification rather than an exam based qualification.
  • Edexcel HND Diploma in Business Level 5
  • Pearson BTEC in Singapore (Level 5) is a vocational qualification in its own right, but is also an alternative pathway to university. It is an intensive program that runs for 15 months and is equivalent to the first two years of a university honours degree.
